# daily-reflection-treee

I designed this reflection flow based on how a person naturally thinks at the end of the day.

First, I ask how the day felt to understand control.
Then I check how they reacted — whether they took responsibility or felt stuck.

Next, I check contribution — whether they helped others or expected something.

Finally, I check focus — whether they were thinking only about themselves or also about others.

Each answer leads to a fixed next step. Same answers always give the same result.

At the end, the system gives a short summary to help the user reflect on their day.
